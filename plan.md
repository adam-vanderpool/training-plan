## Race Day Strategy

**Swim (1.2 mi / ~1,930m):** Start outside/back to avoid contact. Easy first 200m. Minimal kick. Target 2:03-2:08/100m. Expected ~42-46 min.

**Bike (56 mi):** First 20 min ride 5-10 bpm below target HR. Target HR 130-140 throughout. Eat within first 15 min and every 20-25 min after. Expected ~3:35-3:45.

**Run (13.1 mi):** First 2 km will feel strange — normal. Lock into 9:15/mi. Push final 5 km if feeling good. Expected ~2:00-2:10.

**Nutrition:**
- Pre-race (2.5 hrs before): 100-150g carbs (oatmeal + banana + toast)
- Bike: 1 gel every 25 min, 500-700ml fluid/hr, start eating at mile 5
- Run: Gel + water at alternating aid stations, cola in the back half
